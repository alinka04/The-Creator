The Seven Chakras

Root Chakra (Muladhara)

Located at the base of the spine, the root chakra represents our foundation and feeling of being grounded. When balanced, you feel secure and stable.

Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)

Located in the lower abdomen, this chakra connects to our creativity and sexual energy. When balanced, you feel creative and passionate.

Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

Located in the upper abdomen, this chakra is our source of personal power. When balanced, you feel confident and in control.

Heart Chakra (Anahata)

Located at the heart center, this chakra represents our ability to love and connect. When balanced, you feel compassionate and empathetic.

Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

Located at the throat, this chakra represents our ability to communicate. When balanced, you express yourself clearly and truthfully.

Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

Located between the eyes, this chakra is our center of intuition. When balanced, you have good insight and trust your inner wisdom.

Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

Located at the crown of the head, this chakra connects us to divine energy. When balanced, you feel spiritually connected and enlightened.
