Our Story

Mimi & August was born from a passion for creating beautiful, handmade items that bring positive energy and comfort to everyday life. Each piece is crafted with intention and love, using natural materials and mindful processes.

Our mission is to create products that not only look beautiful but also carry a sense of warmth and inspiration. We believe in the power of handmade items to transform spaces and uplift spirits.

Every creation in our collection tells a story and is infused with the positive energy we put into our craft. We hope our pieces bring as much joy to your home as they bring to us in making them.
