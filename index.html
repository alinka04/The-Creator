The Seven Chakras

Energy centers that regulate all parts of our bodily system

Root Chakra (Muladhara)

Located at the base of the spine, the root chakra represents our foundation and feeling of being grounded. When balanced, you feel secure and stable. Imbalances can manifest as anxiety, fear, or financial issues.

Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)

Located in the lower abdomen, this chakra connects to our creativity and sexual energy. When balanced, you feel creative and passionate. Blockages may result in emotional instability or creative blocks.

Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

Located in the upper abdomen, this chakra is our source of personal power. When balanced, you feel confident and in control. Imbalances can cause low self-esteem or digestive issues.

Heart Chakra (Anahata)

Located at the heart center, this chakra represents our ability to love and connect. When balanced, you feel compassionate and empathetic. Blockages may manifest as loneliness or inability to forgive.

Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

Located at the throat, this chakra represents our ability to communicate. When balanced, you express yourself clearly and truthfully. Imbalances can cause communication issues or fear of speaking up.

Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

Located between the eyes, this chakra is our center of intuition. When balanced, you have good insight and trust your inner wisdom. Blockages may result in lack of clarity or connection to intuition.

Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

Located at the crown of the head, this chakra connects us to divine energy. When balanced, you feel spiritually connected and enlightened. Imbalances can cause feelings of isolation or spiritual skepticism.
